Who I am, Professionally

Trevin began his education journey in April, 2022

I am a student at Brigham Young University's Marriott School of Business. Armed with a 3.50 GPA and a burning curiosity for the world of finance, I delved into core business principles, financial analysis, and valuation methodologies. This academic expedition equipped me with the tools to navigate financial statements, decipher market trends, and make informed investment decisions.

But my quest for knowledge didn't stop there. I craved a community of like-minded adventurers, so I joined the Finance Society. Here, I found myself surrounded by peers constantly seeking the latest updates and charting new territories within the ever-evolving financial landscape.

However, theory alone wasn't enough. I longed to apply my newfound skills in the real world. My internship at Heirloom Restaurant Group became my training ground. Here, I honed my analytical skills, wielding financial forecasting like a sword to predict profits and losses for multiple entities. This experience instilled in me the importance of meticulous analysis and data-driven decision making crucial weapons in any aspiring financial warrior's arsenal.

I wasn't afraid to step outside the comfort zone of finance. My experience as a Head Server at Station 22 revealed my leadership prowess. Here, I optimized schedules and spearheaded training initiatives, ultimately increasing customer satisfaction by a remarkable 18%! This experience honed my ability to manage my time effectively, prioritize tasks, and demonstrate unwavering work ethic under pressure.

But my hunger for diverse challenges didn't stop there. My role at Western Bag Co. propelled me into the exciting world of market analysis and brand strategy. I spearheaded the creation of a winning brand strategy, leading to a significant increase in their social media followers. This experience highlighted my ability to analyze trends, develop impactful solutions, and track their success; all crucial skills for navigating the ever-changing marketplace.
